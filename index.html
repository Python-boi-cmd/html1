Nurburgring 1976 Overview

On August 1st, 1976, the Nurburgring Grand Prix was hosted in Nordschelife Germany. The winner, James Hunt, won after 14 laps on the Nurburgring.

Niki Laudas crash

This race had a extremely unexpected event, on lap 2, after Niki Lauda, driving for Ferrari at the time had a near-fatal crash at a fast left hand kink heading into Bergwerk.

Overview of the crash

Niki Lauda's car caught flames and was stuck for 55 seconds in burning flames. Niki Lauda entered a coma that was about 3 to 4 days. He suffered sever burns and made a full recovery after his near-fatal crash at the Nurburgring. He was still recovering and went to a race in a bandage.
