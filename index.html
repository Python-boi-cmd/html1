Nurburgring 1976 Overview

On August 1st, 1976, the Nordschelife Germany hosted the Nurburgring Grand Prix. The winner, James Hunt, won after 14 laps on the Nurburgring.

Niki Lauda's crash

This race had an extremely unexpected event. On lap 2, Niki Lauda, driving for Ferrari at the time, had a near-fatal crash at a fast left-hand kink heading into Bergwerk.

Overview of the crash

Niki Lauda's car caught flames and he was stuck for 55 seconds in burning flames. He entered a coma that lasted about 3 to 4 days. He suffered severe burns and made a full recovery after his near-fatal crash at the Nurburgring. He was still recovering and went to a race in a bandage.
